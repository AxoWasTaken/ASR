Welcome

Welcome to the official ASR webpage! The Axos Socialist Republic is a Minecraft empire based on the server REDACTED. Founded on June 15th 2025 by Lordos Axos, or AxoFX. The capital of the ASR is Axford, one of the largest cities on the server. Well known for being far more advanced than its counterparts in organization, innovation, and leadership.

Ranking

The ASR has a complex ranking system based around legions further broken down into divisions. At the very top are the Lord, Admiral, and Alligant General; together they lead the ASR to a brighter, socialist future.

Below them are the Militant Director and the Civil Director, who use scientific research to benefit the public and the military. Then there are the Generals — three in total — each leading a legion.
