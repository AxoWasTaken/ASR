Plans for new ASR office building | 24/1/2026 | Military

The Military have recently revealed plans to construct a large new office building in the southern district of Axford. The building will occupy an entire block and will house all the Military projects, Civil projects, and main-branch projects. Given the current situation of Civilians filling the gaps which the stressing government have not been able to do, this building and the officials working in it will be able to relive the heavy work from the people. There are currently no objections to the construction of this building however the cost of its construction may take a chunk out of the negligible civil service funding. There are also some reservations from the government around the amount of space required may be used better for residential zones given the rapidly increasing population of 60. Its predicted by the Sentry that in the next 2 weeks the population may vastly increase above 100 if the country, Hillside, joins the ASR. Axford runway may also be housed on the site to save space, the Community Policing (main branch) have not commented on their relocation to the building but the projects that choose to relocate will be given higher funding to cope with the growing civilian population. The Lord has also announced that the ASR is to increase its military size and strength in the next few days to prepare for a large "dynamic push" into unexplored indigenous land between Brokenwood, Axford, and Ft. Razal.

Axian Republic Presidential Election | 19/1/2026 | The Sentry

Recently the Lord has opened an election for a President of Axia. Generally not understood, the ASR consists of other nations/republics, then below that are Prefectures of nations, then provinces are the same level but disconnected. The president, will be the president of all current prefectures and provinces under the Axian Republic, however not other nations part of the ASR. Currently MOUSE_bootless is leading with 75% of votes and in charge of the liberal democratic party [LDP]. Her opponent, _tobuy2549, is 25% of votes and leading his own unnamed party. The LDP has made several promises to make the legal system more fair for all people under the ASR, get the public rights to more land, and make Axia more tidy and presentable. The Lord has actually voted for the LDP party "Personally, I'm voting for Mouse, she has good intention and enough favor to do the people good, in this day and age we'd be lucky to have someone like her.", yet the Admiral and Allegiant General have not yet voted and we will be expecting their votes within the next few days.
