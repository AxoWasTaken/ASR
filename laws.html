ASR Law

This document covers the legal system of the Axos Socialist Republic and any other entities under the same administration.

Civil Offences

These laws cover smaller non-criminal offences, such as trespassing or stealing lower-level goods.

Trespassing

  • Anyone may enter another’s property, but they must leave if asked by the property owner.
  • Refusing to leave another’s property is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • Killing or harming a trespasser over 10 damage points is prohibited and may result in greater punishment than the trespasser.
  • Entering a restricted union zone without permission from at least a commander is prohibited and will result in punishment.

Freedom of Speech

  • Insulting the Lord is only prohibited if made in serious sense, not if made as part of a joke or sketch.
  • Insulting the Admiral is always prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • Insulting a general is always prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • Insulting a commander is only prohibited if made in a serious sense.
  • Insulting a soldier is only prohibited if made in a serious sense.
  • Insulting a civilian or equal no matter rank is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • Insulting a person of lower rank is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • Using the words Skibidi, Alpha (as class), Beta (as class), Sigma (as class), Omega (as class) or other brain‑rot words is prohibited.
  • Racism and sexism are strictly prohibited and will most definitely result in punishment.
  • Leaking classified information or media is strictly prohibited and will result in severe punishment.

Building

  • Building inappropriate structures resembling a Swastika or a male reproductive organ is strictly prohibited. The Soviet hammer and sickle is an exception.
  • Flying the flag of an official enemy of the state is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • Buildings must not exceed 10 blocks without a permit from the Lord.
  • Extending or branching a road adjacent to your property cannot be classed or named without a permit.
  • Constructing a cellar or basement is prohibited if there is already a man‑made chamber or Redstone cable.
  • Mining under your property is prohibited without a permit.
  • Extending or branching an adjacent railway, canal, or ice track is prohibited; isolated ones on your property are allowed.
  • Littering on public land or another’s property is prohibited and may result in punishment.

Transport

  • Check there are no carts before crossing railways; you hold full liability for any accidents.
  • Blocking or damaging the ice tracks is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • Trying to sail boats on R‑class roads is prohibited but cannot be punished.

Redstone

  • Building flying machines on your property requires a permit.
  • Using wireless transmission requires a permit.
  • Projects using noteblocks or jukeboxes require a permit.
  • Projects that contain explosives are strictly prohibited.
  • Lag machines are strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.

Criminal Offences

These laws cover larger offences such as murder or terrorism.

Murder

  • Killing another player is strictly prohibited without general rank and good reason. PvP competitions also require a permit.
  • Inspiring, ordering or bribing another player to kill is strictly prohibited and punished equally to the murder.
  • Indirectly killing (bombs, pitfalls, etc.) is strictly prohibited and will result in severe punishment.
  • Killing villagers is prohibited and may result in great punishment.

Terrorism

  • Placing a TNT cart on the railway is strictly prohibited and always treated as terrorism.
  • Intentionally jamming telegraph lines is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • Sending flying machines to crash into structures is strictly prohibited.
  • Planting landmines without a permit is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • Burning down any building without a permit is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • Kidnapping any player is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • Making, buying, selling, or owning explosive substances is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.

Theft

  • Stealing an item from another player without their permission is prohibited.
  • Stealing money from another player is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • Stealing the Lord’s crown is strictly prohibited and may result in severe punishment.

Fraud

  • Stealing the identity of another player is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • Forging money, permits, ASR documents, fossils, large ore veins, ancient structures, or shipwrecks is strictly prohibited.
  • Pretending to have fought in the Rubicona War is strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.

Protection

  • Cutting down trees in a protected area is strictly prohibited.
  • Killing a protected creature or plant is strictly prohibited.
  • Removing lava from a protected area is strictly prohibited.
  • Damaging a protected site is strictly prohibited.
  • Mining or building in a protected area is strictly prohibited.

Treason

  • Protest and counter‑protest are allowed unless they turn violent.
  • Disobeying orders from commanders is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • Disobeying orders from generals is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • Disobeying orders from the Allegiant General, Admiral, or Lord is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • Starting a riot is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • Becoming a rogue against the ASR is strictly prohibited and may result in severe punishment.
  • Attempting or succeeding in splitting the ASR is strictly prohibited and may result in severe punishment for all sides.
